The Gordon Ramsay Predicament

You walked into your house after a long day at work and suddenly Gordon Ramsay is sitting in your kitchen. Not believing your eyes, you rubbed your eyes and blinked multiple times to no avail.

The Gordon Ramsay, the one and only man who berates people with the word 'Donkey'. The man himself is sitting there, beckoning you over. "I'm waiting for my meal, where is it lad?" he gestures.

You have a choice, to present him a slab of raw chicken or an english breakfast with extra baked beans.

What is going to be your choice?
