A Breakfast a day, keeps Gordon at bay

You remembered reading that Gordon likes a good English breakfast with extra baked beans, so that is exactly what you whipped up with the little amount of energy you have after work and the sheer passion for cooking.

After plating the dish and making sure you remember to dish out that extra baked beans, you earnestly delivered the dish to Gordon in hopes of satisfying his palate.

Gordon examined your dish and started to try out the dish. After a seemingly long and uncomfortable silence in the air, he finally looks up and said:

"Oh my, what an astonishing dish you have made. Brilliant."

Now you think to yourself, why stop here when you can impress Gordon more?

Return to the kitchen and whip up another specialty of yours Chief.
